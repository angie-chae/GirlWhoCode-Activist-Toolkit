General Information

What is Climate Change?

Climate change is the long-term shifts in weather and temperature in the world. Over the past 100 to 200 years, humans have been the main contributor in the increase of climate change across the world.

What is the difference between climate change and global warming?

Global warming is the rise of temperature globally due to greenhouse gases. However, climate change is the long-term changes in climate such temperatures and percipitation.
